MKIT 2024

MKIT will be the biggest Italian Mechanical Keyboard meetup since 2018. The event is open to everyone, enthusiasts and new to the MK hobby.

Bring your keyboards with you and share with other your experience and knowledge!

WHEN

October 12 2024

2:00PM-7:00PM

The date has been decided with the community in order to accomodate as much members as possible, considering other events around Europe.

Why 2:00PM? We are Italian, we often have lunch around 12:00AM and 1:00PM :P

WHERE

Via Carlo Boucheron 15 bis/B, Torino

Thanks to Gabriel for hosting the 2024 meetup in their studios. The location is easy to reach from public transportation, near bus stops, Porta Susa Train Station and Metro.

HOW

Tickets available HERE

[Available from August 5th 12:00am Italian timezone]

Each ticket grants entrance to the venue, badge, stickers and a spot for a maximum of 2 keyboards.
Since the space is not large enough to accomodate an indefinite number of attendees we wanted to be prepared, a limited number of 50 tickets are available for purchase.
The cost of the ticket will cover all the expenses needed to make this event as pleasant as possible for everyone.
