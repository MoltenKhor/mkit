WTF is MKIT?!

MKIT is an excuse to eat in good company, that's it.üçù

After years of inactivity we found an actual excuse to get together again since 2018, after pandemic stopped everything.

From the Community for the Community

MKIT was born from community member who wanted to get reunited, share ideas and spend a nice day with people who relate with the same obsession. It's a way for the community to be stronger and grow.

For Enthusiast

Don't let your nice boards be seen only from your closet's dust. Share your experience, knowledge and flex those nice rectangles. MKIT is the perfect opportunity to show to others your nice collection. Share your experience, knowledge and what you have learnt from years of lubing, soldering and typing, someone will be happy to learn from you.

For New explorers

You just joined a keyboard chat, wonder what the hell we're talking about and can't understand what's the perfect switch for you, the best layout, form factor or keycaps profile? Well this is the perfect opportunity to try so many option that you may find your new favourite build! It's your opportunity to learn from experts, touch with your hands how switches feel and sound and maybe even take a lubing lesson on site.

For Makers

Our community has many designers, makers, engineers with aswesome ideas and some times projects that will never see light if they get abandoned under the desk. Bring your projects, prototypes or drawings. Share with others your ideas and get instant feedback or even find collaborators who are willing to bring your project to life.

For Vendors

This is the opportunity for any business to get known to the community, show what you sell or make and build a stronger customer base learning what people are looking to buy.
